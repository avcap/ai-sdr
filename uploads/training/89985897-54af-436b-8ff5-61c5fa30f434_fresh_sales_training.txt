AI SDR Training Manual

Sales Methodologies:
1. SPIN Selling - Focus on Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-payoff
2. Challenger Sale - Teach, tailor, take control  
3. Consultative Selling - Understand customer needs first

Target Audience:
- Decision makers: CTOs, VPs of Engineering, IT Directors
- Pain points: Manual processes, inefficiency, high costs
- Decision process: Technical evaluation, ROI analysis, security review

Sales Philosophy:
Combining technology with human insight, personalization with scale, and persistence with patience to provide value, build relationships, and solve problems for prospects.

Practical Advice:
- Research prospects thoroughly before outreach
- Use data-driven insights to personalize messages
- Keep communications concise and value-focused
- Follow up consistently but not aggressively
- Leverage social proof and case studies
