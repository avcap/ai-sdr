## Products

### TechCorp AI Assistant
Our flagship product is an AI-powered business assistant that helps companies automate routine tasks, analyze data, and make informed decisions.

**Key Features:**
- Natural language processing for document analysis
- Automated workflow creation
- Real-time data insights
- Integration with 100+ business tools

**Target Customers:** Mid-market companies (100-1000 employees) looking to streamline operations

**Pricing:** $99/user/month with volume discounts available

### TechCorp Analytics Pro
Advanced analytics platform that provides deep insights into business performance and customer behavior.

**Key Features:**
- Predictive analytics
- Custom dashboard creation
- Automated reporting
- Machine learning insights

**Target Customers:** Enterprise companies (1000+ employees) with complex data needs

**Pricing:** Custom pricing based on data volume and features
